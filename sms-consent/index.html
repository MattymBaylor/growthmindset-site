About Our Messaging Program

Growth Mindset AI ("we," "us," "our") offers an SMS messaging program to communicate with home service business owners and prospective customers. Our messages include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Follow-up information requested during phone conversations
  • Service updates and scheduling communications
  • Links to resources, demos, and booking pages you've asked for

How You Opt In

You consent to receive SMS messages from Growth Mindset AI by one or more of the following methods:

  • Verbal consent during a phone call — When speaking with our team or AI assistant, you may request information via text. By asking for a text, you consent to receive that message and related follow-ups.
  • Website form submission — When you submit your phone number through a form on growthmindset.ai, smartvoiceagents.io, or any Growth Mindset AI landing page, you consent to receive SMS communications.
  • Texting START — You can opt in at any time by texting START to any of our messaging numbers.

By opting in, you agree to receive recurring automated text messages from Growth Mindset AI at the phone number you provided. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with our services. Typically, you'll receive 1–5 messages per month. During active service onboarding or appointment scheduling, frequency may temporarily increase.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Emailing us at matt@growthmindset.ai with your phone number and the word "unsubscribe"

After texting STOP, you'll receive a one-time confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you opt back in by texting START.

Help

For help or questions about our messaging program, text HELP to any of our messaging numbers, or contact us at matt@growthmindset.ai.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan. We are not responsible for any charges from your carrier.

Carriers Supported

Our messaging program is available on all major U.S. wireless carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others.

Privacy

Your phone number and personal information are kept confidential. We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Information collected through SMS (including phone numbers) will only be used to deliver the messages you've consented to receive and to improve our services.
