## Rules
- Never send more than 3 texts per lead per week
- Always include the demo number (239-259-9975) — it's the product, not a sales number
- Keep texts under 160 characters where possible (above sequences are slightly over — trim in Retell/N8N)
- Stop sequence immediately if they reply STOP or any variant
- If they reply with a question, route to Matt for personal response
