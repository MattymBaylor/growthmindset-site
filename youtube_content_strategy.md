# YouTube Shorts Content Strategy — Growth Mindset AI
## 4-Week Plan | Two Tracks | Goal: First 10 Clients

---

## The Two Tracks

**Track A: Contractor-facing** — Gets you clients. Direct pain-point content aimed at home service business owners.
**Track B: AI audience** — Builds your brand, grows subscribers, attracts future inbound leads and collaborators.

Post ratio: **3 contractor : 2 AI audience** per week minimum.

---

## The Core Formula for Every Contractor Short

1. **Hook (0–3 sec):** A number, a loss, or a question they feel personally
2. **Pain (3–15 sec):** Make it vivid and local
3. **Reveal (15–45 sec):** Show the solution — your voice, Samantha, or a demo
4. **CTA (last 5 sec):** One action only
