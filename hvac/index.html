She's trained to identify emergency situations — no heat in winter, no cooling in extreme heat, system-down calls. She captures all the information, reassures the caller, and immediately texts you so you can decide whether to respond right away or first thing in the morning.

Most contractors are live within 48 hours of the onboarding call. We handle all the training and configuration. You review, request any tweaks, and approve. Then you forward your line and she goes to work.
