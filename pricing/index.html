Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really a free trial? +

Yes. 14 days, fully functional, no credit card required. Your AI agent will be live and answering calls within 24 hours of signing up. If it's not for you, walk away — no questions asked.

Are there any per-minute or per-call charges? +

No. $397/mo is flat. Unlimited inbound calls, unlimited minutes. We don't nickel-and-dime you based on volume. One price, everything included.

How long does setup take? +

About 24 hours. You tell us about your business (5 minutes), we build and configure your AI agent, you review and approve, then forward your calls. Most clients are live the next business day.

Will callers know it's AI? +

Most don't. The voice is natural and conversational, trained on your brand's tone. It handles real conversations — not robotic scripts. We've had clients tell us their customers thought they hired a new receptionist.

What if I need to cancel? +

Cancel anytime. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no retention games. Email us or click one button. We keep it simple because the product speaks for itself.

Do you integrate with my existing tools? +

Yes. Calendar booking via Cal.com, CRM logging to Google Sheets (or your preferred system), Slack notifications, SMS via your existing provider. Growth and Enterprise plans include custom integrations.

What's the ROI? +

The average missed call costs a home services business $1,200. At $397/mo, one booked job pays for the entire month. Most clients capture 10+ additional leads per month that would have gone to voicemail.
