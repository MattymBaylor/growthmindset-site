FAQ

Questions? Here are the honest answers.

Everything business owners ask before getting started.

Does it actually sound human, or is it obviously a robot?
It sounds natural. Your agent is trained specifically on your business — your services, your tone, your FAQs. Most callers assume they're talking to a real person on your team. You can call our live demo line at (239) 259-9975 and hear it yourself before you decide.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live within 24 hours of the onboarding call. We handle all the training and configuration. You review, request any tweaks, approve, then forward your number. That's it.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You keep your existing number. We set up a dedicated forwarding number that routes through your AI agent. You can toggle it on or off anytime — your number stays yours.
What happens during true emergencies or upset customers?
Your agent is trained to identify urgent situations. It captures all the information, reassures the caller, and immediately texts or escalates to you. For upset customers, you define the handoff rules — we set it up however you want it handled.
What about pricing questions — what does it say?
You decide. We can train it to give ranges, tell callers pricing is given after a free estimate, or steer away from pricing entirely and just book the appointment. Your business, your rules.
Is there a contract?
No contracts. Month-to-month. Start with the 14-day free trial — if it's not worth it, cancel before the trial ends and you pay nothing. We're confident once you see the first week of calls, you'll stay.
What if I serve multiple areas or have multiple service lines?
No problem. We train your agent on your full service footprint — all service types, all coverage areas. Whether you run one location or cover a full region, your agent knows where and what you do.
