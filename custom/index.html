FAQ

Got questions?

Here's everything business owners ask before signing up.

Does she actually sound natural, or is it obvious she's a robot?
She sounds natural. She's trained specifically for YOUR business — your services, your process, your FAQs. Customers regularly assume they're talking to a real person from your team. You can call the demo line at (239) 259-9975 and judge for yourself.
What happens if a call is a true emergency?
She handles it. We train her on your exact escalation protocol — whether that's texting you immediately, transferring to an on-call number, or dispatching a vendor directly. She doesn't just take a message and hope for the best.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live within 48 hours of the onboarding call. We handle all the training and configuration. You review, request any tweaks, and approve. Then you forward your line and she goes to work.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You keep your existing number. We set up a forwarding number that routes through your AI receptionist. You can turn it on or off anytime, and your existing number stays yours.
What does she do when someone asks about pricing?
That's up to you. We can train her to give general ranges, tell callers pricing is provided after a free estimate, or avoid the topic entirely and just book the appointment. You decide how you want it handled.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contracts. It's month-to-month. Start with the 14-day free trial. If it's not worth it, cancel before the trial ends and you pay nothing. We're confident once you see the first week of calls, you'll stay.
What if I have multiple team members or locations?
No problem. We train her on your full operation — who handles what, how you want calls routed, and what your scheduling looks like. Whether you're a solo operator or a growing team, she adapts to how you work.
